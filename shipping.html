Shipping Policy

  •          Products are returnable within the applicable return window if you’ve received them in a condition that's physically broken, has missing components or accessories, defective, or different from their description on the product detail page on freshcatch.info. If you report a problem with your Smartphone, Tablet, Laptop, Television, we may schedule a technician visit to your location.

    Subject to Freshcatch.info Home Services Terms and Conditions, a resolution will be provided to support the technician's analysis report.

    Returns will be processed only if it is determined that the merchandise wasn't broken while in your possession; the product is not different from what was shipped to you; the product comes in original condition (with brand’s/manufacturer's box, MRP tag intact, user manual, warranty card, and all accessories).

    If you would like to return a device that stores any personal data, please ensure that you have removed all such personal data from the device before returning. Freshcatch shall not be liable in any manner for any misuse or usage of such data.

    Products may not be eligible for return in some cases, including cases of buyer's remorse such as incorrect model or color of the product ordered or incorrect product ordered.

    If the replacement request is placed and the seller doesn't have the exact same product available, we'll provide a refund.

    Products purchased by international customers are not eligible for returns.

    CUSTOMER SERVICE


             Our responsibility enables us to work closely with each client. When you purchase an item or when we ship your order, the deal isn't finished; it is only completed when you are completely satisfied. We will provide you with the best quality merchandise at the best possible cost.

    PAYMENT METHOD


    To manage payment methods:

    In Your Account, select Payment options. Do one of the following:

             To add a payment method, select the relevant link under the payment method that you want to add. To edit or remove a payment method, select the drop-down arrow beside the relevant payment method and select Edit or Remove. Follow the on-screen instructions. You can also add a new credit/debit card while placing an order, on the Select a Payment Method page. These details will be saved to your account and will be surfaced as a saved credit/debit card when you place your next Freshcatch order.

    You'll be notified at checkout if we're unable to ship specific items to the address you've chosen. Please also check the product detail pages for any item-specific shipping restrictions.

    To print an invoice:

    Go to Your Orders.

    In the menu, select the appropriate dates to change.

    Scroll to find the order, and select read Invoice. Select print this page for your records, or use your browser's print button.

    If you believe that the product is not in good condition, or if the packaging is tampered with or damaged, before accepting delivery of the goods, please refuse to take delivery of the package, and call our Customer Care at +91 9940786660 or write an email to survay@freshcatchglobal.com mentioning your order reference number. We shall make our best efforts to ensure that a replacement delivery is made to you at the earliest.

    Please note all items (including gifts) will be shipped with an invoice mentioning the price, as per Indian Tax Regulations.
